Reality Brief

About

Nationalism, sensationalism and the propaganda model cause many people to consume a dangerously biased assortment of news. Using the miracles of the internet, Reality Brief reduces a degree of bias by automatically reporting every instance in the world of a variety of predetermined types of events.

More specifically, Reality Brief uses the Wikimedia API to pull current information from crowdsourced lists. It also uses the Sigimera API to pull natural disaster data from gdacs.org.
